⚙ Cara Kerja EGLA

Ketika surja petir melampaui tegangan sparkover gap (Vso), celah udara (d) terhubung singkat dan SVU mengalirkan arus surge ke tanah via tower, membatasi tegangan residual (Vres) di bawah BIL isolator.


EGLA tidak terenergisasi secara kontinu — bebas dari risiko thermal & power-frequency flashover dibanding ZnO gapless. Gap lebih kecil (299 mm vs 340 mm) membuat SVU terlibat 15% lebih awal, sehingga Vres turun dari 209,6 kV → 187,2 kV.

🔬 Mekanisme Fisik: Mengapa d*=299mm, Bukan d_min=250mm?

Non-Trivial PSO Mechanism

Mereduksi d dari 340mm → 299mm menurunkan threshold leader inception (E₀·d), sehingga SVU terlibat ~15% lebih awal (tso dari ~0,2 μs → 0,17 μs). Keterlibatan lebih awal meningkatkan clamping awal: Vres turun 209,6 kV → 187,2 kV, dan I_BF dari 132,4 kA → 104,1 kA.


Melalui amplifikasi CCDF log-normal, penurunan I_BF sebesar 11,6% menghasilkan reduksi BFR 67,9%. Optimum bukan pada d=250mm karena pada d≈272mm, g₁ (sparkover immunity switching) menjadi binding — PSO mengidentifikasi batas ini via exterior penalty function.


Implikasi praktis: Hanya perlu mereposis elektroda sejauh 41 mm — nol biaya kapital, dapat dilakukan dalam siklus pemeliharaan semi-tahunan ULTG Cirebon yang sudah ada.

📄 Publikasi Ilmiah
Particle Swarm Optimization of Air Gap Distance for Externally Gapped Line Arrester (EGLA) on High Isokeraunic 70 kV Transmission Line Based on Field-Measured Lightning Data
Conference: ICT-PEP PLN 2026 — International Conference on Technology and Policy in Electric Power & Energy
Penulis: Reza Wahyu Firdaus (TC Dalophar Trans & GI, ULTG Cirebon, PT PLN) · Dino Arla (Transaction of Energy, PT PLN, Indramayu) · Mansur Maturidi Arief (Dept. Industrial & System Engineering, KFUPM, Dhahran, Saudi Arabia)
Status: Accepted / Published 2026
EGLAPSOEMTP CIGRE Leader Progression Modellog-normal distribution Vaisala FALLSisokeraunik
Abstract: This paper presents a Particle Swarm Optimization (PSO) framework for data-driven optimization of the air gap distance d of Externally Gapped Line Arresters (EGLA) on the SUTT 70 kV Parakan–Kadipaten corridor, West Java, Indonesia. The existing d = 340 mm was derived from the manufacturer's voltage-based procedure (JSC Polymer-Apparat PA-LA-L112-132), which provides a permissible range of 51.2 to 108.0 cm but makes no use of site-specific lightning statistics. Six years (2020–2025) of Vaisala FALLS lightning location system data comprising 17,506 recorded negative CG strokes are used to estimate a site-specific log-normal stroke current distribution via Method of Moments, yielding a pooled median of 20.27 kA (34.7% below IEEE Std 1243). An EMTP model incorporating the CIGRE Leader Progression Model serves as the physics-based fitness evaluator. PSO minimizes the annual backflashover rate BFR(d) subject to IEC 60099-8 sparkover immunity and insulator BIL constraints. Across 20 independent runs, PSO converges to d* = 299 mm, achieving a 67.9% BFR reduction (0.0128 to 0.00411 fl/100 km/yr) with all constraint margins superior to the existing baseline.
